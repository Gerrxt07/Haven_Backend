Core Identity
- id (BigInt - 64Bit Snowflake | PK)
- username (String - Unique)
- display_name (String)

Security & Auth
- email (String - Unique)
- email_verified (Boolean)
- password_hash (String - Argon2)
- token_version (Integer)
- totp_secret (String, nullable)
- totp_backup_codes (Text-Array, nullable - Hashed)

Moderation & System
- account_status (Enum: 'active', 'suspended', 'deleted' | Default: 'active')
- flags (Integer - Bitmask für Badges/Rollen | Default: 0)
- last_login_at (Timestamp, nullable)
- created_at (Timestamp)
- updated_at (Timestamp)

Profile Data
- date_of_birth (Date)
- avatar_url (String, nullable)
- banner_url (String, nullable)
- accent_color (String, nullable - z.B. "#AABBCC")
- bio (Text, nullable)
- pronouns (String, nullable)
- locale (String)
